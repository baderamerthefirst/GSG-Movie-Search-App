Dunkrik (2017)

“Dunkirk” is a 2017 war film by Christopher Nolan that portrays the evacuation of Allied soldiers from Dunkirk during World War II. It uses three intersecting timelines to illustrate the bravery and struggle of those involved in the evacuation, emphasizing the chaos and heroism of the event.
